The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) Program was started in the year 2009 with an intake of 60. As the demand became more the intake has been increased to 120 in 2012. Excellent infrastructure in terms of class rooms, Laboratories and Research facilities are in place. The programme is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum, Karnataka State, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

Department has a set of highly experienced team of faculty members having excellent academic records, proficiency and right mix of Industry and academic exposures. Excellent infrastructure, state-of-the-art computers equipped with the latest advanced technology wherein student centric learning takes place.

We believe in imparting concept-oriented subject knowledge through a high quality pedagogy which is supplemented with hands on practical training. The department has a well stacked library for the students and staff members. Innovative teaching - learning methodologies, excellent academic exposure, soft skills and technical training that suit for the present day needs of industry and academia are adopted.


VISION
To instill profound knowledge, ethical values and technical skills to students in the field of computer applications to meet challenges of industry and society.

MISSION
M1 Create pedagogic ambience of learning, innovative thinking among students to meet the challenges of computing.
M2: Enhance research in computer science and applications.
M3: Develop professionals with ethical values, contributing to the development of society.
